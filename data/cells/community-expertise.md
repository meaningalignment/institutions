# Universities, licensing boards & professional associations

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How a community certifies who is qualified to practice and gatekeeps entry to a field.

### Examples

- University degrees
- State medical boards
- Bar exams
- ABA accreditation
- Professional engineering licenses

### How AGI breaks them

- No body to credential — what gets the license?
- Practice happens at scales accreditation never imagined.
- Continuing-ed assumes a person who learns over years.
- Malpractice with no human practitioner to sanction.
