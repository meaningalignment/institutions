# Technical standards bodies (W3C, IEEE)

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How an industry or community settles on shared technical standards no single firm controls.

### Examples

- W3C
- IEEE
- IETF RFCs
- Unicode Consortium
- Bluetooth SIG

### How AGI breaks them

- Standardization cycles are years; agent practice changes monthly.
- Forks and dialects multiply faster than spec writers can converge.
- Whoever ships the most agents sets the de facto standard.
- Spec interpretation is itself outsourced to agents.
