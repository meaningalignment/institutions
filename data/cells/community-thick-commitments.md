# Mission-driven orgs

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How a community organizes itself around a shared substantive purpose that members opt into and live out.

### Examples

- WWF
- Patagonia
- The Vatican
- Médecins Sans Frontières
- Greenpeace

### How AGI breaks them

- Living-out the mission requires a body and a life.
- Agents simulate participation without inhabiting it.
- Thick commitments don't survive prompt updates.
- Membership rituals don't bind a fleet.
