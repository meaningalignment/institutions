# Mentorship & apprenticeship

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How one party transmits judgment and tacit skill to another over time.

### Examples

- Master-apprentice
- PhD advising
- Coaching relationships
- Therapeutic supervision
- Surgical residency

### How AGI breaks them

- No years-long arc to grow into judgment.
- An agent can mimic a mentor's style without inheriting their stake.
- Principals can't tell skilled-from-glib at agent volume.
- Tacit knowledge gets compiled into prompts and traded.
