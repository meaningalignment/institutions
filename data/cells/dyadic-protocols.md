# Handshake protocols & shared formats

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How two parties establish a shared way of exchanging information so they can transact at all.

### Examples

- Email (SMTP)
- TLS handshake
- EDI for B2B orders
- Shared file formats (PDF/CSV)
- Phone-call etiquette

### How AGI breaks them

- Agents invent ad-hoc protocols faster than they can be standardized.
- Different vendors' agents don't share a wire format for intent.
- Protocol upgrades happen mid-conversation without notice.
- Authentication of who-is-who is fuzzy across stacks.
