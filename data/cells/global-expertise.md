# Expert panels & assessment bodies (IPCC, WHO)

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How the world establishes a shared authoritative reading of complex transnational evidence.

### Examples

- IPCC
- WHO
- IAEA
- FAO assessments
- Cochrane systematic reviews

### How AGI breaks them

- Scientific outputs can be agent-fabricated at scale.
- Consensus processes are slower than agent-generated counter-evidence.
- Whose model produces the assessment matters politically.
- Authoritative readings can be A/B tested against publics.
