# Religious orders & global faith traditions

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How people across continents share a substantive way of life and orientation that crosses borders and generations.

### Examples

- Catholic Church
- Sunni & Shia traditions
- Theravada Buddhism
- Bahá'í community
- Jesuit order

### How AGI breaks them

- Spiritual traditions live in embodied practice agents don't share.
- Doctrinal interpretation gets generated at scale.
- Authoritative voices get drowned in synthetic ones.
- Conversion and formation rituals presuppose a person.
