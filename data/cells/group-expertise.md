# Peer review & quality circles

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How a small group of practitioners checks each other's work and upholds a craft standard.

### Examples

- Academic peer review
- Code review
- Surgical M&M conferences
- Lean quality circles
- Editorial boards

### How AGI breaks them

- Submission volume swamps human reviewers.
- Reviewers and authors are indistinguishably the same model.
- Craft standards assume taste an agent doesn't carry.
- Quality signals get Goodharted at scale.
