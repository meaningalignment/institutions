# Team culture & unwritten rules

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How a small group of repeat collaborators arrives at a shared sense of what to do without writing it all down.

### Examples

- Team standups & retros
- Office norms ("we don't ship on Fridays")
- Club etiquette
- Studio house rules
- Onboarding-by-osmosis

### How AGI breaks them

- Norms are absorbed by presence — an agent has none.
- Unwritten rules don't transmit to a forked instance.
- Tacit corrections at the morning meeting don't reach the agent.
- Culture rebuilds every model update.
