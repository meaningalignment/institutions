# Committee voting & prediction markets

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How a small group with mixed views picks one course of action from many.

### Examples

- Robert's Rules of Order
- Board votes
- Jury deliberation
- Internal prediction markets
- Consensus circles

### How AGI breaks them

- Agents can be sybil-spawned to swing a vote.
- Persuasion at machine scale skews deliberation.
- Whose preference — agent's or principal's — counts?
- Markets get arbitraged by faster agents.
