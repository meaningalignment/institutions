# Spectrum auctions, cap-and-trade & tax policy

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How a state shapes private behavior at population scale by structuring payoffs.

### Examples

- FCC spectrum auctions
- Carbon cap-and-trade
- Income tax brackets
- Pigouvian taxes
- R&D tax credits

### How AGI breaks them

- Auctions get optimally bid by agents instantly.
- Tax avoidance can be auto-discovered.
- Cap-and-trade markets get arbitraged in milliseconds.
- Policy timing assumes humans react slowly — agents don't.
