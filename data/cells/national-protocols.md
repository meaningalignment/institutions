# National standards agencies (NIST, DIN)

## At a glance

### Coordination challenge

How a country fixes shared technical and measurement standards everyone in the economy depends on.

### Examples

- NIST (US)
- DIN (Germany)
- BSI (UK)
- ANSI
- Underwriters Laboratories

### How AGI breaks them

- Standards bodies move slower than agent-driven product cycles.
- Agents read and apply specs in ways the spec didn't anticipate.
- Conformance testing scales poorly to model-based systems.
- Measurement assumes a stable artifact — agents are not stable.
